How to install?

Glad you asked! Just browse all the cool maps below and hit download. After that extract the containts of .zip file to your Minecraft's save location. For Mac, in the Finder, from the Go menu, select 'Go to Folder', then type: ~/Library/Application Support/minecraft, and click Go, for Linux, since ~ is your home directory, so location would be /home/YOURNAME/.minecraft/, and for Windows press Win+R, then type %appdata%\.minecraft.
